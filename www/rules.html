Blackjack Rules

The goal is to beat the dealer by having a hand value closer to 21 without going over.

• Number cards (2–10) = face value
• Face cards (J, Q, K) = 10
• Ace = 1 or 11 (whichever helps more)
• Blackjack (Ace + 10-value card) pays 3:2 (or 6:5 in some games)

Player options: Hit, Stand, Double Down, Split (pairs), Surrender (late, if allowed)

Dealer must hit until 17 or higher (soft 17 rules vary).
