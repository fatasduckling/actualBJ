Index Plays & Deviations (Illustrious 18 + Fab 4)

Deviations are small changes from basic strategy based on the True Count (TC). They add 0.5–1% extra edge when counting.

How Deviations Work

At specific True Counts, basic strategy isn't optimal. Use these index plays to deviate:

  • Rule: Deviate only if TC ≥ index number shown
  • Illustrious 18: 18 most valuable hard hand deviations (stand or double instead of hit)
  • Fab 4: 4 key surrender deviations
  • Insurance: Take at TC +3 or higher (separate from basic strategy)

Memorize these – they pay off big over thousands of hands!
